Monster Combat

Start Game:
- enter your username
- pick your character
- click join

Level:
- click the monster to attack it
- you must get the monsters health down to 0 before the time runs out
- if you do it you will have the option to increase the amount of damage you do or go to the next level
- as you go through the levels the monsters health will increase but the time you have to do it in will stay the same

End Game:
- if you run out of time the game will end
- you can then add your score to the high scores list

Score:
- you will get # points for each level
- bonus points are awarded based on how much time
